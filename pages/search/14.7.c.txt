If it is considered that the proceedings on a specified
item of business on the agenda of the day shall be
concluded on the day itself, it can be transacted being
exempted from the operation of Sub-rule (6) and (7)
of Rule 14 by the adoption of a motion moved by the
Leader of the House or Minister in that respect. The
motion, which shall read as “Business under item No.
……….in the agenda shall be transacted having being
exempted from the provisions of Rule No. 14(5) and
14(6)” shall not be debated. If that motion is adopted,
business can be transacted accordingly.

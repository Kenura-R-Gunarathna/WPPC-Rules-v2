It shall be the duty of the Chief Government Whip to
facilitate affairs of the Council by organizing the
Government party with regard to motions before the
Council and take action to maintain the quorum of the
groups of the Government party members and to
assist the Chairman to obtain views of the leaders of
all political parties and independent groups of the
Government party representing the Council in order to
discuss and take decisions in matters important for the
continuance and functioning of the Council.

The role of the Inquiry Committee on all the
Authorities, Institutions established by a Provincial
Council statute should be to;<br><br>
(1) Present recommendations to the Council after
inspecting about operations of the said authorities to
discern whether they function in accordance to the
objectives and goals stated in the statute,<br><br>
(2) To present recommendations to the relevant minister
about productivity and efficiency improvement and
prevention of waste in projects implemented by the
said authorities and,<br><br>
(3) (c)Having considered the complaints received by
Hon. Members and public with regard to the functions
performed by the said authorities, to present the
recommendations to Council that deem necessary.

The quorum of the Council as required by
Clause 10 (4)<sup>13</sup> of the Act shall be one third of the
total number of members of the Council. If at any
time, the absence of a quorum is noticed by the
Chairman or is brought to his attention by any other
member; he shall order the division bells to be rung.
If at the expiration of five minutes the quorum is not
present, the Chairman shall in accordance with Clause
10 (5)<sup>14</sup> of the Act, adjourn the Council or suspend the
meeting until there is a quorum. In counting a
quorum, the Chairman shall be included.

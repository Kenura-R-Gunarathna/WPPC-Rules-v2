The powers entrusted to Chairmen of the Committees
originate under the powers of the Chairman, and in
any occasion when witnesses and documents are
summoned before the said Committee if the said
persons violate those summoning orders, it shall be
considered as breach of privileges of the Provincial
Council. In such an occasion making the relevant
officers or persons countable for those violations of
privileges shall be conducted as per provisions of the
Establishment Code and the existing law.

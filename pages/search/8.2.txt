When the office of Chairman or Deputy Chairman is
vacant or both become unable to preside in the
Council meeting, a member from the aforesaid panel
of members should preside to execute Chairman's
duties as per Paragraph 7(3)<sup>7</sup>
of the Act or act in
Chairman's office as per Paragraph 7(4)<sup>8</sup>
of the Act
according to the order of the said panel.

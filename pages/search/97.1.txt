The main role of the Provincial Revenue Promotion
Committee should be to;<br><br>
(a) Identifying revenue avenues collectable by Provincial
Council ,<br><br>
(b) Provide instructions and guidance to formulate
statutes and laws that should be formed to collect
revenue through the said revenue avenues.<br><br>
(c) Inquiring about progress in revenue collection and
giving recommendations to make their collection
efficient.

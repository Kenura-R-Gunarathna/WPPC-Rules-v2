The Provincial Council shall not proceed upon the
consideration of any motion, statute or amendment
thereto authorizing the disposal or imposition of
charges upon the Provincial Council Fund or any
other funds of the Provincial Council, for the
imposition of any tax or repeal a tax existing
contemporarily, augmentation or reduction of any tax
for the time being in force to increase or decrease a
tax unless introduced by a Minister who shall, before
making such motion, introducing such statute or
moving such amendment, signify to the Provincial
Council that the approval of the Board of Ministers
has been received.

The foremost duty of the Vigilant Committee shall be
as follows:-<br><br>
(a) To forward recommendations to the relevant
Minister in respect of the fruitfulness,
improving the efficiency and avoiding the
wastage of the projects implemented out of the
Provincial Council provisions.<br><br>
(b) To examine the complaints received to
Chairman from the members and the public
regarding such projects and to intimate the
relevant Minister through recommendations if
it is deemed necessary.

When an answer is not ready for a question stated in
the agenda, the relevant Minister or, if the relevant
Minister is absent due to unavoidable reason, another
Minister on his behalf or the Leader of the House may
request time for answering. Requests for time made
at any one time shall not exceed a period of two
months. However, in making requests for time from
time to time, the maximum period for which time can
be requested shall be limited to 03 months.
